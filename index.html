Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children who are 3-4 years old and provides a play-based introduction to learning. Our experienced teachers use a variety of activities and games to help children develop important skills, such as socialization, language, and fine motor skills.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students in grades K-5 and provides a rigorous and engaging education that addresses the intellectual, emotional, and social needs of each child. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students and promote deep understanding.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves students in grades 6-8 and provides a challenging and supportive education that prepares students for high school and beyond. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students and promote deep understanding.
