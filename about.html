Our Mission & History

The Friends School of Atlanta was founded in 1999 by a group of families seeking a values-based education that integrated academic rigor with social responsibility. Our campus, nestled in the Buckhead neighborhood, has become a vibrant community of learners, thinkers, and doers.

Our teaching approach is rooted in the Quaker principles of mutual respect, critical thinking, and collaborative problem-solving. Our experienced faculty engage students in hands-on learning experiences, encouraging creativity, inquiry, and critical thinking.

Beyond the classroom, our students discover their passions and talents through various extracurricular activities, including sports, music, art, and community service. Our programs foster a sense of belonging, empathy, and social responsibility, preparing students to make a positive impact in the world.
