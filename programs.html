Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

The Pre-Kindergarten program at The Friends School of Atlanta is designed for curious and energetic 3-5 year olds. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that encourages social, emotional, and cognitive growth.

📚

Elementary

The Elementary program at The Friends School of Atlanta serves students in grades K-4. Our engaging curriculum integrates academics with hands-on learning experiences, fostering a love of learning and a sense of community.

📊

Middle/Upper

The Middle/Upper program at The Friends School of Atlanta serves students in grades 5-8. Our experienced teachers challenge students to explore their interests, develop their talents, and prepare for high school and beyond.

💻

STEM

The Friends School of Atlanta's STEM program offers students a rigorous and engaging curriculum that integrates science, technology, engineering, and math. Our experienced teachers inspire students to explore, innovate, and problem-solve.

🎭

Arts

The Arts program at The Friends School of Atlanta provides students with opportunities to explore their creativity through music, art, drama, and dance. Our experienced teachers foster a love of the arts, promoting self-expression and community engagement.

🏀

Athletics

The Friends School of Atlanta's Athletics program offers students a range of sports and activities that promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Our experienced coaches inspire students to strive for excellence, both on and off the field.
