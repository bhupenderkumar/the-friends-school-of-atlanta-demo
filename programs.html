Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4, where they'll engage in play-based learning, music, and art activities to develop their social, emotional, and cognitive skills.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, children in grades K-5 will explore a range of subjects, including language arts, math, science, and social studies, in a supportive and engaging environment.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper school programs serve students in grades 6-8, where they'll develop their critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills through a range of academic and extracurricular activities.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program is designed to foster creativity, innovation, and critical thinking in students, where they'll engage in hands-on activities, coding, and project-based learning to develop their STEM skills.

🎭

Arts

At The Friends School of Atlanta, we believe that the arts are essential to a well-rounded education. Our Arts program offers a range of activities, including music, drama, and visual arts, to help our students express themselves creatively.

🏀

Athletics

We believe that sports are an important part of a child's education, where they'll develop teamwork, discipline, and physical skills through a range of sports and physical activities.
